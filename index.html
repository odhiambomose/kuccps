About Us

The Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service (KUCCPS) is a State Coorporation that provides career guidance and selects students for admission to universities...

Read More...

189 institutions have signed up for central student placement coordinated by KUCCPS. These include 68 universities and 121 colleges offering up to 990 programmes.

Read More...

The Placement Service is governed by the Placement Board which is constituted as per the provisions of the Universities Act, 2012.

Read More...

The Placement Service has put in place a highly professional, dedicated and motivated secretariat to implement its programmes.

Read More...

The Placement Service is an equal opportunity employer. Vacancies are advertised in national media and other channels approved by the Government of Kenya.

Read More...

Our strategy is driven by customer experience, business process re-engineering, financial sustainability and institutional capacity.

Vision

An equitable, fair and efficient placement service

Mission

To nurture careers through advisory services and placement to education and training institutions

Core Values

Customer Centricity | Access and Equity | Innovativeness | Teamwork | Integrity
